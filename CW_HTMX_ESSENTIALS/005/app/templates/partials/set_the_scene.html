Welcome to the Interactive LEGO Assembly Workshop!

Imagine you have a giant box of LEGO bricks. Your goal is to build a cool creation on your baseplate. The traditional way of building a web page is like taking your entire creation apart and rebuilding it from scratch every time you want to change just one single brick. It's slow and inefficient.

HTMX changes the game. Think of HTMX as a set of magic instructions. Instead of rebuilding everything, you can simply point to a brick on your model (like a solid wall) and say, "Swap this for a window piece." The server—your box of bricks—sends you just the window piece, and it magically snaps into place.

In this lesson, you'll be the master builder. You'll use HTMX to swap parts, add new bricks, and even pull a specific, rare piece (a drawbridge!) from a completely different instruction manual. Let's start building!
