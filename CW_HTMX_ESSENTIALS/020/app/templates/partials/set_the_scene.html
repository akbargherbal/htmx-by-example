Welcome to the Chemistry Lab!

In this lab, we're not just mixing chemicals; we're exploring the core principles of modern web development. Think of your web browser as a high-tech laboratory and HTMX as your set of advanced instruments.

Every action you take—like mixing chemicals with `hx-post`—is an experiment. The server processes your request and sends back a result, just like a chemical reaction. When the beaker is bubbling, it's our visual indicator that a request is in progress.

Sometimes, a reaction triggers a lab-wide alert (an `HX-Trigger` header), or a sensitive monitor needs to be checked constantly (polling). This lesson will show you how HTMX makes these complex, asynchronous interactions feel simple, precise, and powerful.
