Welcome to the Vintage Mailroom Workspace!

Imagine you're a clerk in a bustling, old-fashioned office mailroom. Your job is to handle inter-office memos. Every button click is like sending a request to a different department.

Sometimes, your request is for a file that simply doesn't exist (a 404 Not Found error). Other times, you might try to contact a department that's unexpectedly closed for the day (a 500 Server Error). And occasionally, a department moves, so your mail must be automatically rerouted to the new address (an HX-Redirect).

This lesson explores how HTMX, like a seasoned mailroom supervisor, expertly handles all these scenarios—lost mail, closed offices, and address changes—ensuring the right message gets to the right place, or that you're properly notified when it can't.
