Welcome to the Smart Kitchen!

This demo uses a "kitchen" analogy to make HTMX concepts feel tangible. Here's the setup:

  • Chef's Station (Your Controls): This is your webpage, the frontend. The buttons and forms are your way of interacting.
  • The Kitchen (The Server): This is the invisible backend. It receives your requests and prepares a response.
  • Dining Table (Server Response): This is where the result of your action is "served" and displayed on the page.

You're not writing complex JavaScript; you're just making requests. Let's see how simple HTMX makes it.
