Welcome to the Retro Jukebox Vibe!

Picture a classic diner. In the corner, a beautiful, glowing jukebox waits. That's our application, and HTMX is the magic that makes it work without the complex wiring of modern JavaScript frameworks.

In this lesson, every action you take mimics a real-world jukebox interaction. "Inserting a coin" isn't just a button click; it's an hx-post request that tells the server to change its state and "unlock" the song selectors. Previewing a song uses hx-get to fetch details and display them without "spending your credit."

Finally, selecting a song uses another hx-post to add your choice to the queue. HTMX handles swapping these pieces of the user interface, making the experience feel dynamic and responsive, just like a real jukebox responding to your selections.
