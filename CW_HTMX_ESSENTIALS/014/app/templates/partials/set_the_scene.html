Welcome to the The Digital Home Renovation Workshop!

Think of a modern webpage not as a static painting, but as a room you can dynamically renovate. In this workshop, you're the lead contractor, and HTMX is your powerful set of tools.

We'll explore how HTMX attributes act like instructions for your digital crew. You'll see how to install a new window pane (`hx-swap`), go to the hardware store for a full door assembly but only bring back the doorknob (`hx-select`), and provide exact measurements for a custom cabinet (`hx-include`). This hands-on analogy makes abstract concepts tangible, showing you how to update parts of your webpage without having to rebuild the entire room.
