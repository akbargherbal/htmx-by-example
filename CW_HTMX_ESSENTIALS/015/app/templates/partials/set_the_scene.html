Welcome to the Vintage Public Library Archives!

In this lesson, we're stepping into the quiet, hallowed halls of a grand library. Our goal is to request a rare, archived book—a task that requires interacting with the librarian.

The "Archived Book Request" form is your digital request slip. Filling it out and submitting it is like handing your request to the librarian. The "Librarian's Desk" is where you'll see the result of your request.

Notice how this interaction happens instantly, without the entire page having to reload. This seamless experience is powered by HTMX, acting as our hyper-efficient digital librarian.
